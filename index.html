Core Services

AI Sourcing

Leverage AI to search databases and social platforms for candidates that match your job description. Receive a structured list of prospects with match reasons.

Outreach Automation

Create personalised outreach campaigns powered by AI. Automate follow‑ups, track open and reply rates and sync metrics back to your dashboard.

AI Screening

Use AI to parse resumes, assess skill fit and culture alignment, and generate concise candidate summaries with interview question suggestions.

Interview Coordination

Automate scheduling across calendars and time zones. Send invites, reminders and thank‑you notes seamlessly via Calendly and Zapier integrations.

Reporting & Dashboard

Visualise your entire pipeline in one place. Track sourcing volume, outreach metrics, screening scores, interview feedback and hires from a single dashboard.

How It Works

  1. Submit your requirements: Fill out our intake form with the role, location and key criteria.
  2. AI agents go to work: Our sourcing and screening agents search multiple data sources, score candidates and prepare outreach.
  3. Engage candidates: Personalised emails are sent automatically and interested candidates are scheduled for interviews.
  4. Make better hires: Review candidate summaries, interview notes and analytics in your dashboard to make data‑driven hiring decisions.
