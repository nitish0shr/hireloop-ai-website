End‑to‑End Workflow

  1. Upload JD: Submit your role via our form or drag‑and‑drop.
  2. JD Analyzer: GPT parses the role & generates search strings, scoring criteria & outreach copy.
  3. Sourcing Bot: Searches Apollo, LinkedIn & Clay for top matches and loads them into Airtable.
  4. Outreach Bot: Sends personalised emails, monitors open/reply rates and adjusts sequences.
  5. Resume Screener: AI evaluates resumes & replies, scores and shortlists candidates.
  6. Interview Coordination: Calendly invites, confirmations & reminders are sent automatically.
  7. Dashboard & Reports: View your pipeline, scorecards and match summaries in real time.
